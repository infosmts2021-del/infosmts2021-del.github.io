# First Time in Europe: A Beginner's Guide

Planning your first European adventure? Here's what you need to know.

## Best Duration

For first-time visitors, 2-3 weeks is ideal. This gives you enough time to:
- Fly and recover from jet lag
- Visit 2-3 major cities
- Explore nearby regions
- Avoid feeling rushed

## Must-Visit Cities

### London, England
- Historic landmarks
- World-class museums
- Vibrant food scene

### Paris, France
- Romantic atmosphere
- Art and culture
- Excellent cuisine

### Amsterdam, Netherlands
- Charming canals
- Cycling culture
- Progressive atmosphere

## Getting Around

- **Flights**: Budget airlines for longer distances
- **Trains**: Eurail Pass for flexible travel
- **Buses**: Cheapest option
- **Car Rental**: For countryside exploration

## Budget Tips

- Stay in hostels or budget hotels
- Eat like locals (street food, markets)
- Use free walking tours
- Visit museums on free-entry days

Enjoy your European adventure!
