# Weather Dashboard Guide

Our new weather dashboard helps you plan your travels by providing current weather conditions and 5-day forecasts.

## Features

### Current Weather
- Real-time temperature and conditions
- "Feels Like" temperature
- Humidity levels
- Wind speed
- Atmospheric pressure
- Weather emoji for quick visual reference

### 5-Day Forecast
- Daily high and low temperatures
- Weather conditions for each day
- Visual weather indicators

## How to Use

1. **Visit the Dashboard**: Go to the [Weather Dashboard](/weather/)
2. **View Current Conditions**: See real-time weather for San Francisco
3. **Plan Ahead**: Check the 5-day forecast to prepare for your travels

## Weather Codes Explained

### Clear Weather
- ☀️ Clear Sky
- ⛅ Mainly Clear / Partly Cloudy
- ☁️ Overcast

### Precipitation
- 🌧️ Rain or Drizzle
- ❄️ Snow
- 🌨️ Snow Showers
- ⛈️ Thunderstorms

### Other
- 🌫️ Foggy Conditions

## API Information

Our weather dashboard uses the **Open-Meteo API**, a free, open-source weather API that requires no API key.

### Benefits
- ✅ No registration required
- ✅ High accuracy
- ✅ Global coverage
- ✅ Free tier available
- ✅ Respects user privacy

## Travel Planning Tips

1. **Check 5-Day Forecast**: Plan activities based on expected weather
2. **Pack Appropriately**: Bring weather-appropriate clothing
3. **Plan Indoor Activities**: For rainy days (🌧️, ⛈️)
4. **Schedule Outdoor Activities**: For clear days (☀️, ⛅)

## Temperature Guides

### Cold Weather (Below 10°C)
- Pack warm jackets and layers
- Wear hats and gloves
- Use moisturizer for dry skin

### Comfortable Weather (10-20°C)
- Light layers recommended
- Suitable for most outdoor activities
- Perfect for sightseeing

### Warm Weather (Above 20°C)
- Light, breathable clothing
- Use sunscreen
- Stay hydrated
- Sunglasses and hat recommended
