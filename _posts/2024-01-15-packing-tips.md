# Ultimate Packing Tips for International Travel

Packing can be stressful, but with these tips, you'll travel light and smart.

## Essential Items

1. **Passport & Documents**
   - Passport (valid for 6+ months)
   - Travel insurance
   - Flight tickets/confirmations
   - Hotel reservations

2. **Electronics**
   - Phone and charger
   - Universal power adapter
   - Portable battery
   - Camera

3. **Clothing**
   - Mix and match pieces
   - Comfortable walking shoes
   - Light layers
   - Weather-appropriate gear

## Pro Tips

- Roll clothes instead of folding
- Use compression bags
- Pack only what you need
- Keep valuables in carry-on
- Wear bulky items on the plane

Happy travels!
