# Popular Destinations

## Europe

### Paris, France
- Best time to visit: April-June, September-October
- Duration: 3-5 days
- Highlights: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame

### Barcelona, Spain
- Best time to visit: May-June, September-October
- Duration: 2-4 days
- Highlights: Sagrada Familia, Park Güell, Las Ramblas

## Asia

### Tokyo, Japan
- Best time to visit: March-May, September-November
- Duration: 4-6 days
- Highlights: Senso-ji Temple, Shibuya Crossing, Mount Fuji

### Bangkok, Thailand
- Best time to visit: November-February
- Duration: 3-5 days
- Highlights: Grand Palace, Wat Phra Kaew, Floating Markets

## Americas

### New York, USA
- Best time to visit: September-November, April-May
- Duration: 3-5 days
- Highlights: Statue of Liberty, Times Square, Central Park

### Machu Picchu, Peru
- Best time to visit: May-September
- Duration: 2-3 days
- Highlights: Ancient Inca ruins, Inca Trail, Sacred Valley

## Africa

### Safari, Kenya
- Best time to visit: June-October, December-March
- Duration: 4-7 days
- Highlights: Big Five, Serengeti, Masai Mara

## Oceania

### Sydney, Australia
- Best time to visit: September-November, March-May
- Duration: 3-5 days
- Highlights: Opera House, Bondi Beach, Harbour Bridge

### Bali, Indonesia
- Best time to visit: April-October
- Duration: 5-7 days
- Highlights: Temples, Rice Terraces, Beaches
